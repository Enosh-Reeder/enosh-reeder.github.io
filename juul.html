Project Summary

As a mechatronics engineer on the internal tools & test team at JUUL Labs, I design, build, and deploy automated systems that accelerate R&D throughput for aerosol-chemistry and device-reliability testing. The work spans mechanical design, embedded electronics, robotics software, and data-science pipelines.

UR10e Robotic Work Cell

  • Engineered a UR10e collaborative-robot work cell integrated with ROS2/MoveIt for repeatable, hands-free sample loading and device actuation during aerosol-chemistry tests.
  • Added Intel RealSense depth cameras for vision-guided pick-and-place, enabling sub-millimeter positional repeatability across device variants.
  • Designed custom end-effectors and fixturing machined in-house; reduced manual operator time per test run by >60%.

AI Session-Analysis Pipeline

  • Built an end-to-end Python pipeline (Pandas, scikit-learn, SQL) that ingests raw puffing-session telemetry, detects anomalies, and classifies device behavior — cutting triage time from days to hours.
  • Integrated FTIR and mass-spectrometer instruments over GPIB/USB, automating data acquisition and correlation with device session logs.
  • Deployed a lightweight ML model for real-time go/no-go quality checks, replacing manual review for >90% of routine test runs.

PCB Design & Nitinol Fixtures

  • Designed multi-layer Altium PCBs for custom test fixtures: USB-C power delivery conditioning, CAN-bus breakout boards, and sensor-data-acquisition front ends.
  • Prototyped nitinol shape-memory-alloy actuators to drive repeatable button-press mechanisms, eliminating wear-related variance over millions of actuation cycles.
  • Fabricated functional-checkout-test (FCT) fixtures used on the manufacturing line to verify electrical and mechanical compliance of finished devices.

In-House Toxicology Rig

  • Collaborated with chemistry team to design and build a closed-loop aerosol exposure rig for in-house cellular toxicology studies, replacing outsourced contract-lab testing and shortening feedback cycles from weeks to days.
  • Integrated peristaltic pumps, mass-flow controllers, and custom impinger assemblies under LabVIEW DAQ control with automated safety interlocks.

Impact

The suite of automated tools developed on this team directly accelerates JUUL's R&D velocity: faster test cycles, richer datasets, and fewer manual touch-points across chemistry, reliability, and regulatory-compliance workflows.
