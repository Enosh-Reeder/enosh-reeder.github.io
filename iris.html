Project Summary

As Senior Mechatronics Engineer at Udelv, I was integral to the team that designed, built, and tested the uPod, a 4000lb & 340ftÂ³ modular robotic cargo storage platform for an autonomous electric delivery vehicle. My focus was on the electromechanical systems that managed the cargo.

Mechanical Design & Systems

  • Created a modular cargo door and shelving system, structure, electronics and sensor housings, and mechanisms to optimize cargo capacity and cost.
  • Researched materials and additives to achieve a product lifetime of millions of cycles.

Friction Analysis & Resolution

  • Determined and resolved areas of high friction on the cargo door system by analyzing oscilloscope data with python scripts.

Conclusion

This project was a comprehensive exercise in mechatronic design for a real-world autonomous vehicle application, successfully balancing cost, durability, and functionality.
