Project Summary

As a Mechatronics Engineer at Transparent Sky, I prototyped a modular, aircraft-mounted gigapixel-class Wide Area Motion Imaging (WAMI) system. The core of the project involved enclosing the complex sensor suite in a custom-designed aerodynamic composite pod.

Design for Manufacturing

The primary challenge was designing the composite pod for both aerodynamic efficiency and manufacturability, ensuring the sensitive imaging equipment was secure and functional during flight.

Mentorship & Leadership

  • Recruited & mentored interns and junior engineers for design & manufacturing roles, fostering a collaborative and productive team environment.

Conclusion

This project successfully delivered a functional WAMI prototype and demonstrated effective team leadership and rapid prototyping skills in an aerospace context.
